# Key Findings: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Common Patterns

1. Most experts use AI for first drafts and content structuring.
2. Human editing remains essential to pass Google E-E-A-T guidelines.
3. Topical authority through dense content clusters is emphasized repeatedly.
4. Content strategy is shifting from standard SEO to GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).

## Frequently Mentioned Tools

- ChatGPT & Claude (For drafting and analysis)
- Surfer SEO & Clearscope (For semantic optimization)
- Ahrefs & Semrush (For tracking and topical mapping)
